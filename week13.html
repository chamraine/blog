Week 13

By Charmaine Cheng  -  January 9th, 2024.

During the previous week, we wrapped up the design phase of the admin page UI. This week, our focus shifted towards ensuring the website's functionality by establishing connections to the Firebase database.

Our initial task involved setting up the "Add Employee" page to seamlessly integrate with both Realtime and Authentication Firebase databases. Authentication Firebase serves the purpose of managing employee authentication, allowing us to retrieve their details through their unique user ID generated upon account creation. Meanwhile, Realtime Firebase is utilized for storing additional employee information such as departments, phone numbers, and roles.

Following the completion of the 'Add Employee' functionality, we utilized a similar approach to develop the 'Update Employee Detail' page. The primary distinction here is the incorporation of 'Delete Records' and 'Update Record' functionalities, allowing administrators to modify the data stored in Firebase as needed.

Additionally, we implemented an "Add Profile Picture" feature, enabling users to upload their profile pictures. These images are stored in the Firebase Storage within the designated "profile picture" folder, where each picture is renamed with the respective user ID in the format "userid.png". This naming convention facilitates easy identification of the pictures associated with each user.

In short, we've improved our website's functionality and user experience by adding the admin page and connecting to Firebase. We're committed to ongoing refinement to better serve our users.
