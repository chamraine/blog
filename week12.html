Week 12

By Charmaine Cheng  -  January 1st, 2024.

During Week 12, we embarked on incorporating feedback from our sponsors and lecturer, which highlighted the absence of an Admin view in our system.

Originally, our platform catered solely to employees and employers, overlooking the necessity for an administrative interface. Consequently, we made the decision to introduce an Admin view to facilitate tasks such as editing, updating, and deleting employee records, along with integrating additional functionalities like leave request approvals or denials.

Our primary focus during this week was on crafting an intuitive and comprehensive user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) for the Admin page. The objective was to present a centralized hub where administrators could effortlessly manage employee records. This included features such as viewing a roster of all employees with pertinent details like names, departments, and profile pictures, enabling administrators to access individual profiles with a single click.

Complementing the UI/UX design, we also revamped the employee details page to offer administrators a streamlined view of comprehensive employee information. This revamped page facilitated easy review, editing, and deletion of individual records as shown below.

Furthermore, we developed an intuitive UI for adding employee records, ensuring administrators could input various details seamlessly.



Overall, our focus was on enhancing the system's functionality and user-friendliness by introducing the Admin view and refining the associated UI/UX elements.
