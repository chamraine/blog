Week 14

By Charmaine Cheng  -  January 15th, 2024.

During Week 14, we took constructive feedback from our lecturer to heart and implemented enhancements to our 'Upload MC' page.

The focal point of these improvements was the addition of an 'Upload MC Image' feature, aiming to elevate the page's functionality. This new capability empowers administrators to validate the authenticity of medical leave claims submitted by employees, thereby ensuring a more robust verification process.

Moreover, we introduced a complementary feature known as the 'Leave Summary.' This innovative functionality provides employees with a centralized view of all their submitted leave requests. Within the 'Leave Summary,' employees gain access to comprehensive details such as the type of leave requested, start and end dates, submission date, and crucially, the approval status. Notably, the approval status defaults to 'Pending,' prompting employees to await administrator approval before finalizing their leave arrangements.

These enhancements represent a significant step forward in optimizing our system's usability and efficiency, ultimately enhancing the experience for both administrators and employees alike.
