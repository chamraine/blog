Week 17

By Charmaine Cheng  -  Feb 5th, 2024.

During Week 17, on the eve of our presentation and demo day, we spent time creating slides for our presentation as well as to rehearse our presentation to ensure that our flow was good. This is because we did not want to make the same mistake as we did when we had our presentation with the AIDC sponsor where it was feedbacked that our presentation was messy.

Before the presentation, we double-checked both our website and app to ensure they were working properly. However, we encountered some bugs, especially on the website. The leave request feature suddenly stopped working just a few minutes before the presentation, even though it had been fine the night before. At that point, the issue seemed to be on sharon's and my laptop, as it worked fine on Vinhee's laptop.

After investigating, we realized there was a problem with pulling from GitHub. I suspect there was a push conflict that caused some code to be replaced, making it not work. Thus, we ended up using Vinhee's laptop for the demo.

We presented our Inception deck slides along with our demo slides. During the demo, there were some issues with admin logging in, possibly due to session problems. Despite these challenges, the presentation and demo were successful overall, especially compared to our initial presentation with AIDC sponsor. We received feedback on areas for improvement, both in my presentation skills and in further enhancing our product.

Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed this module, despite occasional stress when our features didn't work as expected or when I felt completely lost in solving issues. However, having such fantastic teammates made all the difference. Knowing I could rely on them when I lacked confidence in a task kept me motivated and determined.

(best team!!!)
