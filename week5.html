Week 5

By Charmaine Cheng  -  November 15th, 2023.

In Week 5, we participated in a 2.5-day hackathon, where our goal was to advance the development of our prototype and present the results to sponsors on the event's final day.

Before the hackathon kicked off, my teammates and I meticulously planned the features we aimed to complete, adding Session, fix Upload Photo error, and the seamless integration of various project components which inclueded the Website, Camera and Database.

Throughout the hackathon, my primary focus remained on refining the website as most of the features were more or less completed. I addressed issues with the photo uploading feature and implemented necessary validations.

However, a challenge emerged during the development process when attempting to fetch data from the database for the website's employee attendance record.

It was found that the error was due to uncaught reference error: "firebase is not defined."

I went onto google to solve the issue, which Sharon and I managed to solve after many hours.

Concurrently, efforts were dedicated to updating our inception deck, crucial for the final presentation to sponsors on the hackathon's concluding day. In preparation, our team held a call the day before to ensure a polished pitch.

Despite encountering some challenges during the presentation, our demo effectively showcased that our solution successfully met the data collection criteria.


Valuable feedback from sponsors underscored the need to address the disarray in our firebase database, incorporate a upload MC photo in submit MC page, and explore the creation of a mobile application for enhanced convenience.

These insights will guide our efforts in refining the solution during the upcoming two-week break.
